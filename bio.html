Quill's Voice
Diana Hawley

I am an aspiring author who has been writing fiction as a hobby for about five years. This past year, I have set my discipline to prepare for publishing. I favor darker themes and anything with a historic twist. I am also a budding blogger and website designer who is currently pursuing a degree in Technical Communications.

My stories are free to read and hosted on my blog The Indie Horror Show. I also host a site for interactive writing featuring in-character performances from talented writers called The Eclipsed Quill.

I do have a few works for publishing in progress. My first novel, The Black Butterfly will be ready for submission by the end of 2015. I am also working on a co-authored piece entitled Rosenrot Asylum.

I am interested in networking with other dark-themed writers and hunting for literary agents who are looking for submissions in the horror genre.
